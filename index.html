1.A hundred little flames

‘A Hundred Little Flames’ is an out of the box story for many reasons. I particularly liked the way characters strengthen page after page. The letters in the prose add a great substance and the entire setting of the story is described beautifully, be it Kerala or Pondicherry. When 26-year-old Ayan is sent to live with his grumpy old grandfather Gopal Shanker, in a tiny village, in Kerala, he is understandably devastated. What can a sleepy, idyllic village without even Internet connectivity offer a young man?...This is the story line

2.MY LIFE

President A P J was the most loved President of our time! This book portrays Why! His entire journey right from his childhood is an inspiration to dream and turn the dream into reality. Hard work, sincerity, whole hearted dedication and passion to what you do will ensure ones true success in both your personal and professional lives!! You live on in our thoughts Sir!

3.THE ALCHEMIST

You'll know, after reading why this book is the 7th most read book in the world. The story has interesting elements. It has humor, amazement, motivation and most importantly a hard-hitting ridiculous suspense at the end. It may have a higher level of vocabulary, but you would be fine if you have Google handy. A must read. The author has greatly planned the plot.
